Bloch-Kato 1990 give a short exact sequence

\begin{equation*} 0\to \QQ_p\xrightarrow\alpha B_{cris}^{\phi =1} \oplus B_{\dR}^+ \xrightarrow\beta B_{\dR} \to 0\text{,} \end{equation*}

where \(\alpha (x) = (x,x),\beta (x,y) = x-y\text{.}\) Tensor with \(V\) and take Galois cohomology to get

\begin{equation*} 0 \to V^{G_{K_p}} \to D_{cris}(V) ^{\phi =1} \oplus D_{\dR}^+(V) \to D_{\dR}(V)\to H^1(G_{K_p}, V) \to H^1(G_{K_p},V\otimes B_{cris}^{\phi =1}) \to \cdots \end{equation*}

Bloch-Kato show that

\begin{equation*} H^1_e(G_{K_p}, V) = \ker(H^1(G_{K_p}, V) \to H^1(G_{K_p},V\otimes B_{cris}^{\phi =1})) \end{equation*}

is equal to

\begin{equation*} H^1_f(G_{K_p}, V)\text{.} \end{equation*}
