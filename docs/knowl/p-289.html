For a continuous \(p\)-adic representation \(V\) of \(G_{\QQ_p}\text{.}\) We define

\begin{equation*} D_{\dR}(V) = (B_{\dR} \otimes V)^{G_{K_p}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} D^+_{\dR}(V) = (B^+_{\dR} \otimes V)^{G_{K_p}} \end{equation*}

the filtration on \(B_{\dR}\) induces one on \(D_{\dR}(V)\text{.}\)
