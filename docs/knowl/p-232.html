If \(\{\omega _{0}, \ldots, \omega _{2g-1}\}\) is a basis for \(H^1_{\dR}(X)\) with \(\{\omega _0, \ldots, \omega _{g-1}\}\subseteq H^0(X, \Omega ^1)\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \{ (\phi ^*)^n \omega _g, \ldots, (\phi ^*)^n \omega _{2g-1}\} \end{equation*}

is a basis for \(W\) mod \(p^n\) where \(\phi \) is a lift of frobenius.
