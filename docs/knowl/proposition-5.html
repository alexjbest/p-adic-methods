Proposition 2.37.

For all \(l\in \NN\) and every \(w \in \QQ_q\lb x\rb ^{\oplus d_x}\) there exist vectors \(u,v\in \QQ_q\lb x\rb ^{\oplus d_x}\) such that

\begin{equation*} \deg(v) \lt \deg(r) \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \frac{\sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} w_i b_i^0}{r^l} \frac{\diff x}{r} = \diff \left(\frac {\sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} v_i b_i^0}{r^l} \right) + \left(\frac {\sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} u_i b_i^0}{r^{l-1}} \right) \frac{\diff x}{r} \end{equation*}
Proof.
