Theorem 2.129. Chiarellotto-Le Stum 99.

There is an equivalence of categories

\begin{equation*} \mathcal C^\dR(X_{\QQ_p}) \xrightarrow \sim \mathcal C^\rig(\mathcal X_{\FF_p}) \end{equation*}

given by the analytification functor \((\cdot)^\an\text{.}\) For any \(x \in X(\QQ_p)\) with reduction \(\bar x\) we have a canonical isomorphism of fibre functors

\begin{equation*} i_x\colon \bar x^* \circ (\cdot)^\an \simeq x^* \end{equation*}

such that if \(x,y\) belong to the same residue disks the canonical isomorphism \(\tau _{x,y} = i_x\circ i_y\inv \) is given by parallel transport along the connection.
