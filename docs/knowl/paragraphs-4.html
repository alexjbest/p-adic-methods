Infinite pole order reduction:.

For \(i = 0, \ldots, 2g-1\text{,}\) find \(f_{i,\infty} \in \mathcal R \otimes \QQ_p\) such that

\begin{equation*} \phi^\ast \omega_i = df_{i,0} + df_{i,\infty} + H_i \end{equation*}

where \(H_i\) only has poles at point \(P\) above \(\infty\text{,}\) and \(\ord_P(H_i) \geq (\ord_0 (W) - \deg r + 2) e_p\) where \(W = (W^0)^{-1} W^\infty\) and \(e_p\) is the index of ramification of \(x\)-map at \(P\text{.}\)
