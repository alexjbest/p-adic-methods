On 11/12 we defined the \(\QQ\)-pro-unipotent completion. Now say a little more about the \(\QQ_p\)-pro-unipotent completion.

\begin{equation*} \ZZ_p\lb \lb \pi _1^\et (X_{\overline \QQ}, b)_{\QQ_p} \rb \rb = \varprojlim \ZZ_p\lb \pi _1^\et(X_{\overline \QQ}, b)\rb /N \end{equation*}

where the limit is over all finite quotients of \(p\)-power order.
