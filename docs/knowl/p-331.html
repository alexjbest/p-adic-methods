Recall \(\pi_1^\et(X_{\overline \QQ})_{\QQ_p}\) is the \(\QQ_p\)-pro-unipotent completion of \(\pi_1^\et(X_{\overline \QQ})\text{.}\) Let

\begin{equation*} W= \pi_1^\et(X_{\overline \QQ})_{\QQ_p}/ [ \pi_1^\et(X_{\overline \QQ})_{\QQ_p}^{(2)},\pi_1^\et(X_{\overline \QQ})_{\QQ_p}^{(2)}] \end{equation*}

be the quotient by the derived series

\begin{equation*} G^{(0)} = G \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} G^{(n)} = [G^{(n-1)},G^{(n-1)}] \end{equation*}

\(W\) itself has a lower central series filtration

\begin{equation*} W =W^1 \supseteq W^2 \supseteq \cdots W^{n-1} = \lb W,W^n\rb \supseteq \cdots \end{equation*}

and associated quotients \(W_n = W/W^{n+1}\text{.}\)
