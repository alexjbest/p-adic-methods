Let \(\omega_{i_1}, \ldots \omega_{i_n}\) be forms of the second kind, regular at \(P,Q \in X(\QQ_p)\text{.}\)

  1. \(\int_P^P \omega_{i_1} \ldots \omega_{i_n} = 0\)
  2. \(\sum_{\text{all permutations }\sigma} \int_P^Q \omega_{\sigma(i_1)} \ldots \omega_{\sigma(i_n)} = \prod_{j=1}^n \int_P^Q \omega_{i_j}\)
  3. \(\int_P^Q \omega_{i_1} \ldots \omega_{i_n} = (-1)^n \int_Q^P \omega_{i_n} \ldots \omega_{i_1}\)
