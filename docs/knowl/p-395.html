By the lemma, if \(Z\) is a nice correspondence we get a homomorphism

\begin{equation*} c_Z \colon \QQ_p(-1) \to \ker(\bigwedge ^2 H^1_\et(X_{\QQ_p}, \QQ_p) \xrightarrow \cup H^2(X_{\overline \QQ}, \QQ_p)) \end{equation*}

and using that \(U_2\) was a central extension

\begin{equation*} 1 \to \coker(\QQ_p(1) \xrightarrow{\cup^*} \bigwedge^2 V) \to U_2 \to V \to 1 \end{equation*}

take the quotient \(U_Z = U_2/\ker(c_Z^*)\) and we have a short exact sequence

\begin{equation*} 1\to \QQ_p(1) \to U_Z \to V \to 1\text{.} \end{equation*}
