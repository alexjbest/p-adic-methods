The unipotent completion

\begin{equation*} U(E) \end{equation*}

can be realized as the grouplike elements in \(\QQ\lb \lb E\rb \rb \text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} U(E) = \{g \in \QQ \lb \lb E\rb \rb : \Delta (g) = g\otimes g\} \end{equation*}

This defines the \(\QQ\)-points of a pro-algebraic group over \(\QQ\text{.}\)
