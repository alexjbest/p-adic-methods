Proposition 2.35.

Let \(G \in M_{d_x \times d_x} (\ZZ_q \lb x, 1/r\rb )\) denote the matrix s.t.

\begin{equation*} \diff(y^j) = \sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} G_{i+1,j+1} y^i \diff x \end{equation*}

for \(j=0,\ldots, d_x-1\text{.}\) then \(G = M/r\) where \(M\in \Mat_{d_x \times d_x} ( \ZZ_q\lb x\rb )\text{.}\)
