For all \(n \gt 0\) the Hodge filtration on \(A_n^\dR\) is the unique filtration s.t.

  1. \(\Fil^\bullet\) makes \((A_n^\dR, \nabla)\) into a filtered connection.
  2. The natural maps induce a sequence of filtered connections
    \begin{equation*} V_{\dR}^{\otimes n }\otimes \sheaf O_X \to A_n^\dR \to A_{n-1} \to 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
  3. the identity element of \(A_n^\dR(b)\) lies in \(\Fil^0 A_n^\dR(b)\text{.}\)
