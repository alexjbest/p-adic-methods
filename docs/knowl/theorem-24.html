Theorem 2.92. Coates, Kim '10.

Let \(X/\QQ\) be a smooth projective curve of genus \(g \ge 2\) and suppose \(J\) is isogenous over \(\overline \QQ\) to

\begin{equation*} J \sim \prod A_i \end{equation*}

of abelian varieties with \(A_i\) having CM by \(K_i\) of degree \(2 \dim A_i\text{.}\) Then for \(n \gg 0\) have \(X(\QQ_p)_n\) is finite.
