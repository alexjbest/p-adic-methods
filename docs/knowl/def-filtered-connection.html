Definition 2.122. Filtered connections.

A filtered connection \((V, \nabla)\) is a connection on \(X\) together with an exhaustive, descending filtration

\begin{equation*} \cdots \supseteq \Fil^i V \supseteq \Fil^{i+1}V\supseteq \cdots \end{equation*}

satisfying Griffiths transversality

\begin{equation*} \nabla (\Fil^iV) \subseteq \Fil^{i-1} V \otimes \Omega ^1\text{.} \end{equation*}
