Crash course in Bloch-Kato Selmer groups. Let \(V\) be a finite dimensional \(\QQ_q\)-vector space with a continuous action of \(G_{K_v}\text{.}\) Let, \(K_v = \QQ_p\text{.}\) Fontaine defined \(D_\cris (V) = (B_{\cris} \otimes_{\QQ_p} V)^{G_{K_v}}\) for a ring of p-adic periods \(B_{cris}\) we have

\begin{equation*} \dim_{K_v} D_{\cris} ( V) \le \dim_{\QQ_p} V \end{equation*}

and if equality holds we say that \(V\) is crystalline. See Berger, or Caruso, or Andre.
