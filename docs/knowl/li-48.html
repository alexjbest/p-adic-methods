Item 1.

Determine a basis for cohomology. We want to find \(\omega_0, \ldots, \omega_{k-1} \in (E_0 \cap E_\infty)\cap \Omega^1(U)\) such that

  1. \(\{ \omega_0, \ldots, \omega_{k-1} \}\) is a basis of
    \begin{equation*} H^1_\text{rig} \cong (E_0 \cap E_\infty) / d(B_0 \cap B_\infty) \end{equation*}
  2. The class of every element of \((E_0 \cap E_\infty) \cap \Omega^1(U)\) in \(H^1_\text{rig}(U)\) has \(p\)-adically integral coefficients with respect to \(\{ \omega_0, \ldots, \omega_{k-1} \}\text{.}\)
  3. \(\{ \omega_0, \ldots, \omega_{k-1} \}\) is a basis for the kernel of \(\res_0 \oplus \res_\infty\) and hence for the subspace \(H^1_\text{rig}(X)\) of \(H^1_\text{rig}(U)\text{.}\)
