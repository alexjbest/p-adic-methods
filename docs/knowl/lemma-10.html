Lemma 2.107.

Let \(U\) be a quotient of \(U_2\) that is an extension of \(V\) by \(\QQ_p(1)^m\text{,}\) let \(p\) be a prime of good reduction for \(X\text{.}\)

  1. \begin{equation*} \dim \Sel(U)\le \rk J(\QQ) \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \dim H^1_f(G_{\QQ_p}, U) = g + m \end{equation*}
