Let \(p\ge 5\) be a prime of good ordinary reduction for \(E\text{.}\) Let \(0\ne P \in E(\QQ)\text{,}\) then write

\begin{equation*} P = (x(P), y(P))= \left( \frac{a(P)}{d(P)^2},\frac{b(P)}{d(P)^2}\right) \end{equation*}

and assume \((a(P),d(P)) = (b(P),d(P)) = 1\) and \(d \ge 1\text{.}\) \(d(P)\) is the denominator of \(P\text{.}\)
