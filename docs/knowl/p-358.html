The split mixed extension

\begin{equation*} \QQ_p \oplus V_{\dR} \oplus \QQ_p(1) \end{equation*}

has the structure of a filtered \(\phi \)-module as a direct sum. So we choose two further splittings

\begin{equation*} s^\phi \colon \QQ_p \oplus V_{\dR} \oplus \QQ_p(1) \xrightarrow\sim M \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} s^{\Fil} \colon \QQ_p \oplus V_{\dR} \oplus \QQ_p(1) \xrightarrow\sim M \end{equation*}

\(s^\phi \) is frobenius-equivariant and \(s^{\Fil}\) respects the filtrations, \(s^\phi \) is unique and \(s^{\Fil}\) is not.
