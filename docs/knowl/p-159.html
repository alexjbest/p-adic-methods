\(P,Q \in X(\QQ_p)\text{,}\) \(\eta_1, \ldots, \eta_n\) are 1-forms of the 2nd kind, without poles at \(P,Q\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \eta_P^Q \eta_1 \ldots \eta_n = \int_P^Q \eta_1(R_1) \int_P^{R_1} \eta_2(R_2) \ldots \int_P^{R_{n-2}} \eta_{n-1}(R_{n-1}) \int_P^{R_{n-1}} \eta_n \end{equation*}

for dummy variables \(R_i\text{.}\)
