Let \(A\) be an affinoid algebra and \(A^\dagger\) its weak completion. Let \(\bar A = A^\dagger / \pi \text{.}\) The idea behind isocrystals and its relation to iterated Coleman integrals is that the iterated Coleman integral can be thought of as a solution to a certain \(p\)-adic differential equation (with certain additional structure).
