Algorithm for Hodge filtration

  1. Compute local coordinates at each \(x \in D\)
  2. For each \(x \in D\) compute Laurent series expansions \(\omega \) at \(x\) to large precision,
  3. Compute \(\Omega _x\) defined by
    \begin{equation*} \diff \Omega _x = -\omega _x \end{equation*}
  4. solve for \(\eta\) as the unique
    \begin{equation*} \omega _{2g}, \ldots, \omega _{2g + d-2} \end{equation*}
    s.t.
    \begin{equation*} \diff \Omega \end{equation*}
    has residue zero at all \(x\in D\text{.}\)
  5. Solve the system of equations for \(g_x\text{.}\)
