Definition 2.62.

Let \(D \in \Div^0(X)\) then \(\omega _D\) is the unique form of the third kind with

\begin{equation*} \Res(\omega _D) = D \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \Psi(\omega _D)\in W \end{equation*}

recall we fixed

\begin{equation*} H^1_\dR(X) = H^0(X, \Omega _X^1) \oplus W\text{.} \end{equation*}
