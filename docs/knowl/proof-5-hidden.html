Since \(r\) is separable, \(r'\) is invertible in \(\QQ_q\lb x\rb /r\) Check that there exists a unique solution \(v\) to the \(d_x\times d_x\) linear system.

\begin{equation*} \left( \frac{M}{r'} - l I\right)v \equiv \frac{w}{r'} \pmod r \end{equation*}

over \(\QQ_q\lb x\rb /(r)\text{.}\) Then take

\begin{equation*} u = \frac{w - (M - lr'I)v}{r} - \frac{\diff v}{\diff x}\text{.} \end{equation*}
