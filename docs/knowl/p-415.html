Recall: if \(X/\QQ\) is a smooth projective curve of genus \(g \ge 2\) then we assume

\begin{equation*} r = \rk J(\QQ) = g \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \rho = \rk \NS (J_\QQ) \ge 2 \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \overline{J(\QQ)} \subseteq J(\QQ_p) \end{equation*}

is finite index with \(p\) good for \(X\text{.}\) \(X\) has everywhere potential good reduction.
