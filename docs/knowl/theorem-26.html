Theorem 2.105. B.-Dogra.

Let \(X/\QQ\) be a smooth projective curve of genus \(g\ge 2\text{.}\) Let \(r=\rk J(\QQ)\) and

\begin{equation*} \rho = \rho (J_{\QQ}) = \rk \NS(J_{\QQ}) \end{equation*}

suppose \(r \lt g + \rho - 1\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} X(\QQ_p)_2 \end{equation*}

is finite.
