Item 3.
For Coleman integration from a very bad point (a point above \(\infty\) or a point with \(x\)-coordinate such that \(r(x) = 0\)) \(B\text{,}\) split up the integral
\begin{equation*} \int_B^Q \omega_i = \int_B^{B'} \omega_i + \int_{B'}^Q \omega_i \end{equation*}
for \(B'\) a point near the boundary of the residue disk of \(B\text{.}\)
