Let \(X\) be a smooth projective curve over an algebraically closed field \(k = \overline k\) and \(n \ge 1\) be invertible in \(k\) then

\begin{equation*} H^0_\et(X_{\overline k} , \mu _n) = \mu _n \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} H^1_\et(X_{\overline k} , \mu _n) = \Pic^0(X_{\overline k} ) [n]\text{.} \end{equation*}
