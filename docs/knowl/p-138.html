Assumption 1: Let \(W^0 \in \GL_{d_x}(\ZZ_q \lb x,1/r\rb )\text{,}\) \(W^\infty \in \GL_{d_x}(\ZZ_q \lb x,1/x,1/r\rb )\) be matrices such that if we denote

\begin{equation*} b_{j}^0 = \sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} W_{i+1,j+1}^0 y^i \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} b_{j}^\infty = \sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} W_{i+1,j+1}^\infty y^i \end{equation*}

for all \(0 \le j \le d_x - 1\text{.}\) Then \(\{b_j^0 \}_{j=0}^{d_x-1}\) is an integral basis for \(\QQ(x,y) \) over \(\QQ\lb x\rb \text{.}\) \(\{b_j^\infty \}_{j=0}^{d_x-1}\) is an integral basis for \(\QQ(x,y) \) over \(\QQ\lb 1/x\rb \text{.}\)
