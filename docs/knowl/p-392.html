A correspondence \(Z \in \Pic(X\times X)\) is symmetric if there are \(Z_1, Z_2\in \Pic(X)\) such that

\begin{equation*} \tau _* Z = Z + \pi_1^* Z_1 + \pi_2^*Z_2 \end{equation*}

where \(\pi _1, \pi _2\) are the canonical projections

\begin{equation*} X\times X\to X\text{.} \end{equation*}
