In the cases we consider, these are finite dimensional varieties. The coordinate ring

\begin{equation*} \sheaf O(\pi _1^\et(X_{\overline K})_{\QQ_p}) \end{equation*}

is a \(\QQ_p\)-vector space. Recall that for a smooth curve \(X\) we had

\begin{equation*} H_1^\dR(X_{K_p}) \simeq D_{\dR} (H_1^\et(X_{\overline K}, \QQ_p)) \end{equation*}

we may define the de Rham fundamental group

\begin{equation*} \pi _1^\dR(X_{K_p}) \end{equation*}

as

\begin{equation*} \Spec(D_{\dR}(\sheaf O(\pi _1^\et(X_{\overline K})_{\QQ_p})))\text{.} \end{equation*}
