Let \(E/\QQ\) and \(p \ge 5\) be good ordinary, the cyclotomic \(p\)-adic height \(h\) on such points \(P\) in \(E(\QQ)\) is

\begin{equation*} h(P) = \frac 1p \log_p\left(\frac{\sigma (P)}{d(P)}\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}

\(\sigma (P)\) is the \(p\)-adic sigma function associated to \(E/\ZZ_p\text{.}\)
