Given a finitely presented discrete group \(E\text{,}\) take the group algebra

\begin{equation*} \QQ[E] \end{equation*}

complete it with respect to the augmentation ideal \(K\)

\begin{equation*} \QQ\lb \lb E\rb \rb = \varprojlim_n \QQ\lb E\rb /K^n \end{equation*}

the coproduct

\begin{equation*} \Delta \colon \QQ\lb E\rb \to \QQ\lb E\rb \otimes \QQ\lb E\rb \end{equation*}

defined by \(g\mapsto g \otimes g\) takes \(K\) to the ideal

\begin{equation*} K\otimes \QQ\lb E\rb + \QQ\lb E\rb \otimes K \end{equation*}

then there's an induced coproduct on

\begin{equation*} \Delta \colon \QQ\lb \lb E\rb \rb \to \QQ\lb \lb E\rb \rb \hat \otimes \QQ\lb \lb E\rb \rb \text{.} \end{equation*}
