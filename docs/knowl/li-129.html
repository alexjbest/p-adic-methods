\begin{equation*} A = I(x,x_0) ^+ I(b_0, b)^- \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} I^\pm (x_1, x_2) = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \amp 0 \amp 0 \\ \int_{x_1}^{x_2} \omega \amp 1 \amp 0 \\ \int_{x_1}^{x_2} \eta + \int_{x_1}^{x_2} \omega^\transpose \omega Z\omega \amp1 \end{pmatrix} \end{equation*}
solve p-adic differential equation.
