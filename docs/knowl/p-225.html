Let \(T(K)\) be the space of differentials of the third kind on \(X\text{,}\) at most simple poles, integer residues. We have a residue divisor hom

\begin{equation*} \Res \colon T(K) \to \Div^0(X) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \omega \mapsto \Res(\omega ) = \sum_p \Res_p \omega \end{equation*}

we have the short exact sequence

\begin{equation*} 0\to H^0(X, \Omega _X^1) \to T(K) \xrightarrow{\Res}\Div^0(X) \to 0\text{.} \end{equation*}

We're interested in \(T_l(K)\) these are log differentials

\begin{equation*} \frac{\diff f}{f},\,f\in K(X)^\times\text{.} \end{equation*}
