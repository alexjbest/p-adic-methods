Idea.

Given an extension of \(\QQ_p\) by \(W\)

\begin{equation*} 0\to W \to E\to \QQ_p \to 0 \end{equation*}

choose a splitting \(s^\phi \colon \QQ_p\to E\) which is \(\phi \)-invariant. Choose a splitting \(s^{\Fil} \colon \QQ_p\to E\) which respects the filtration. \(s\) chosen earlier. \(s^\phi \) is unique by \(s^{\Fil}\) is determined up to an element of

\begin{equation*} \Fil^0W\text{.} \end{equation*}

So

\begin{equation*} s^\phi - s^{\Fil} \in W \pmod{\Fil^0W} \end{equation*}

is independent of choices.
