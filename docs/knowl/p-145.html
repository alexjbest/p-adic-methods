Define the \(\QQ_q\)-vector spaces

\begin{equation*} E_0 = \left\{\left( \sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} u_i(x) b_i^0\right)\frac{\diff x}r : u \in \QQ_q\lb x\rb ^{\oplus d_x}\right\} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} E_\infty = \left\{\left( \sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} u_i(x,1/x) b_i^\infty \right)\frac{\diff x}r : u \in \QQ_q\lb x,1/x\rb ^{\oplus d_x}\right\} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} B_0 = \left\{\sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} v_i(x) b_i^0 : v\in \QQ_q[x]^{\oplus d_x}\right\} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} B_\infty = \left\{\sum_{i=0}^{d_x-1} v_i(x,1/x) b_i^\infty : v\in \QQ_q[x,1/x]^{\oplus d_x}\right\} \end{equation*}

then \(E_0 \cap E_\infty \) and \(\diff(B_0\cap B_\infty )\) are finite dimensional \(\QQ_q\)-vector spaces and

\begin{equation*} H^1_{rig} (U)\simeq (E_0 \cap E_\infty )/\diff(B_0\cap B_\infty )\text{.} \end{equation*}
