Suppose \(E\) has good ordinary reduction at \(p\text{,}\) let \(\mathcal L_p(E,T)\) be the \(p\)-adic \(L\)-function attached to \(E/\QQ\text{.}\)

  1. \begin{equation*} \ord_{T=0} \mathcal L_p(E,T)=\rk E(\QQ) \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \mathcal L_p^* (E,0) =\frac{ \epsilon_p \prod c_v |\Sha(E/\QQ)|\Reg_p}{\#E(\QQ)_{\text{tors}}^2} \end{equation*}
    the leading coefficient.

\(\epsilon _p = (1-\alpha \inv)^2\) where \(\alpha \) is the unit root of \(x^2 - a_p x + p=0\text{.}\)
