Example 2.45.

Let \(\phi(P), \phi(Q)\) be images of \(P, Q \in X(\QQ_p)\) under Frobenius \(\phi\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation} \int_P^Q \omega_i \omega_k = \int_P^{\phi(P)} \omega_i \omega_k + \int_{\phi(P)}^{\phi(Q)} \omega_i \omega_k + \int_{\phi(Q)}^Q \omega_i \omega_k + \int_P^{\phi(P)} \omega_k \int_{\phi(P)}^Q \omega_i + \int_{\phi(P)}^{\phi(Q)} \omega_k \int_{\phi(Q)}^Q \omega_i\tag{2.1} \end{equation}
